## Something I watched recently

This talk by Rich Hickey keeps coming back to me whenever I think about how we approach problems:

<YouTubeEmbed id="f84n5oFoZBc" title="Hammock Driven Development - Rich Hickey" />

The core idea: step away from the keyboard. Let your background mind work on the problem. I find this especially true for design decisions — sleeping on it almost always leads to a simpler solution.

## Takeaway

Not every idea needs to be a polished essay. Sometimes you just need to hit record and think out loud.
